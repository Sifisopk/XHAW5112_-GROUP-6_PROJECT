About us

Empowering the nation is a company that aims to provide skills training to domestic workers and gardeners. giving them more opportunities to be more marketable and enabling them to earn higher wages or start their own businesses.

The SME is an initiative by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners.

Customers (employers or employees) can select from a range of courses available. They can select more than one course. The more courses the customers select the more discount they receive.

• One course – no discount
• Two courses – 5% discount
• Three courses – 10% discount
• More than three courses – 15% discoun
